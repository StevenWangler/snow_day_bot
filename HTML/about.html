About Blizzard

Welcome!

Blizzard is an innovative, AI powered app that predicts the percentage chance of a snow day for Rockford Public Schools (Rockford, Michigan).

How It Works

Utilizing OpenAI's GPT model and comprehensive weather data, Blizzard predicts snow days based on temperature, wind speed, humidity, and precipitation.

Key Features

  • AI powered predictions
  • Personalized email notifications
  • Custom website
  • Integration with Google Forms to collect email information

Adherence to Policies

Blizzard has context of specific RPS data, making it's predictions more accurate than traditional snow day calculators.
